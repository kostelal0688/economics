Our Mission

The mission of this website is to raise awareness about bus efficiency and fairness. Many people depend on buses every day, and when buses are slow or unreliable, it affects school, work, and daily life. This project supports better bus service so everyone has equal and reliable access to transportation.

Interesting Facts

About 1.3 million people ride the bus every day in NYC.

Many low-income communities depend on buses as their main transportation.

Faster and more reliable buses reduce traffic and pollution.

About

This website was created by a NYC high school student to raise awareness about bus efficiency and equity. Many students, workers, seniors, and low-income families rely on buses as their main transportation.

The goal is to inform the community, promote fair and reliable bus service, and provide resources to take action.
